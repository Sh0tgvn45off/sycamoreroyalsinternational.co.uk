Who We Are

Sycamore Royals is a Christian Not for Profit; Nongovernmental entity currently registered as a Community based organization. It Demonstrates and proclaims the gospel by providing a nexus of Charitable and social enterprise Programs. Sycamore Royals was founded in 2020, by Stephen and Elizabeth Okiror. This was to spur children and families to develop holistically, care for Creation and build sustainable businesses.

Our Vision

Everyone, Everywhere, living a transformed, holistic and flourishing Life.

Our Mission

Sycamore Royals exists to Proclaim and Demonstrate the Gospel of Jesus Christ to Children and their families through holistic social development services, creation care and sustainable businesses.

Our Core Values

Being God and People Centric: We put God and people at the center of what we plan, Do, and Speak.

Integrity: We are accountable to God and man; our private and public affairs are in sync.

Pursuing Excellence: God is of excellence; we will not do any less.

Building Lasting Legacies: We invest for now, next generation and eternity.

Our Approach

Implementation: SR does both direct as well as partnership approach to implementation. We believe in being facilitative as a biblical sycamore tree - enabling people meet God and opportunities for sustainable transformation.

Programs: SR programs adopt a transformational development Approach - holistic in nature taking a whole gospel to a whole person resulting in whole human flourishing.

Meet Stephen

Our History

In 2020 Stephen Okiror got a clear sense of call from God to create a place in his ancestral land (Wera Amuria -Uganda) for children to Worship God, get nurtured as generational leaders and inspired to attain social-economic improvement through transformational interventions. Born and raised in this community to a family of 12, Stephen experienced firsthand deprivation, isolation, and poverty. God impressed on him to nurture children in this community, raise a godly army of Worshippers, and change the course of this community.
