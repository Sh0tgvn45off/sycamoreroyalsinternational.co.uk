Our Events & Programs

Making a difference through community action

Transforming Lives Through Action

At Sycamore Royals International, we believe in practical action that creates lasting change. Our programs are designed to empower children, strengthen families, and build thriving communities.

Current Programs

Educational Support

Providing school supplies, tutoring, and scholarship assistance to ensure every child has access to quality education. We believe education is the foundation for transformation.

Discipleship & Evangelism

Hosting periodic gospel events and discipleship programs to share the message of hope and transformation through Jesus Christ.

Community Development

Implementing sustainable initiatives that improve living standards, create opportunities, and empower local leaders to drive change.

Leadership Training

Mentorship classes designed to raise up the next generation of community leaders who will continue building lasting legacies.

Youth Engagement

Saturday school sessions and sports interactions keep children active, engaged, and connected to positive role models in their community.

Health & Wellness

Basic health education and wellness programs to ensure families have the knowledge and resources for healthy living.
